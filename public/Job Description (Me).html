Job Description

Manufacturing Engineer

  • Create, configure and develop industrial processes.
  • Appraise, measure and analyzes processes.
  • Create and perform expiremental process test with respect to quality and method.
  • Complete process simulations.
  • Execute risk assessments.
  • Improve process capability and production volume while maintaining and improving quality standards.
  • Provide suggestions during incident investigations.
  • Ensure projects are completed on time.
  • Act as the process lead on the studies and projects.
  • Prepare and review project proposal studies and technical reports.
  • Create and manage process documentation and operating instrutions.
  • Develop and implement metrics to monitor and improve performance of processes.
